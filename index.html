ABOUT RANDOM

At RANDOM Matcha Bar, we've created an intimate space where the ancient art of Japanese tea ceremony meets contemporary dining. Our minimalist interior, featuring warm wood tones and carefully curated lighting, provides the perfect backdrop for experiencing premium matcha.

Every element of our space has been thoughtfully designed to create a sense of tranquility and focus. From our custom pendant lighting to our carefully selected ceramics, we've crafted an environment that honors the meditative nature of tea culture while remaining thoroughly modern.

Our team of skilled tea artisans sources only the finest ceremonial grade matcha from traditional farms in Uji, Japan, ensuring each cup delivers an authentic and exceptional experience.
